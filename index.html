👋 about me

The person behind the pages

Hey! I'm Kathrine or Kat — a designer and developer who loves all things landing pages. I've spent the last few years learning what actually makes people click, stay, and come back.

I started building pages for fun, then for friends, then for real clients — and realized I genuinely love helping people launch their ideas online. There's something satisfying about handing someone a page and watching them say "wait, that's actually good."

I'm self-taught, detail-obsessed, and I respond fast. If you've got an idea you want to put on the internet, I'd love to help you do that.
